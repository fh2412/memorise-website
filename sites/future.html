A Glimpse into the Future

It is time to unlock the Potential of Memorise

The journey of Memorise doesn't stop here! We're constantly innovating and developing new features to make capturing, sharing, and reliving your memories an even more enriching experience. Here's a sneak peek at some of the exciting features on the horizon:


Stage 1: Unleash Your Next Adventure with Memorise Activities


Get ready to add a whole new layer of exploration and shared experiences to your Memorise journey! We're excited to unveil our upcoming "Activities" feature, currently in development.


What are Activities?

Activities are curated experiences – either user-generated adventures or offerings from local businesses – designed to spark your creativity and fuel unforgettable memories.



Two Types of Activities


  • Open Adventure Activities: Let your imagination run wild! This category allows users to create and share their own activities, from hiking trails and sports games to local festivals and cultural experiences. These activities are open to everyone, fostering a vibrant community of memory-makers.
  • Curated Adventure Activities: Discover unique experiences offered by local businesses directly within the Memorise app. Imagine booking a kayaking trip or a visit to a historical landmark, all while seamlessly integrating it into a shared memory with friends.



Activities: A Perfect Fit for Memorise


  • Find Inspiration: Browse through a diverse range of activities when seeking ideas for your next memory-making adventure.
  • Enhance Your Memories: Link existing memories to relevant activities, enriching the narrative and context of your shared experiences.
  • Plan the Perfect Outing: Use the activity search and filter options to find the ideal adventure – whether you're looking for a solo escape, a group outing, or something close by.
  • Discover Hidden Gems: Explore user-generated activities and uncover unique local experiences you might have otherwise missed.
  • Get the Full Picture: For Curated Adventure Activities, access detailed information, reviews, and even user-generated memories associated with the activity, allowing you to make informed decisions.


Activities are coming soon to the Memorise app! Stay tuned for further updates and prepare to embark on a whole new level of memory creation with Memorise.



Stage 2: Capture, Share, Relive On-the-Go: The Memorise Mobile App!


The power of Memorise is about to extend beyond your desktop! We're thrilled to announce the upcoming launch of our mobile app, currently in development. This intuitive app will allow you to capture, share, and relive your memories with even greater ease and flexibility.


Effortless Memory Creation

Imagine a world where you can instantly document special moments straight from your phone. With the Memorise mobile app, adding photos and details to your memories is a breeze. Capture a breathtaking sunset on vacation, a birthday celebration with friends, or a heartwarming family gathering – all with just a few taps on your screen.


A World of Inspiration

Memories are meant to be shared and celebrated! The Memorise mobile app features a dedicated feed where you can discover memories created by your friends and family. Get inspired for your next adventure, reminisce about shared experiences, and witness the beauty of life unfolding through the lens of others.


Your Memories, Always at Hand

No matter where life takes you, your precious memories are always within reach. The mobile app provides seamless access to your personal collection of memories. Revisit past experiences, relive cherished moments, and strengthen the bonds with your loved ones – all from the palm of your hand.


Stay tuned for the arrival of the Memorise mobile app! We can't wait for you to experience the freedom and convenience of capturing and sharing memories on-the-go.



Stage 3: Enhance Your Memories: The Memorise Shop & Engaging Competitions


The journey to preserve your precious memories doesn't end with capturing and sharing them within the app. We're excited to unveil stage 3 of Memorise development, introducing two exciting features designed to further enhance your experience:



1. The Memorise Shop: Tangible Keepsakes for Lasting Memories


What if you could hold your favorite memories in your hands, or proudly display them on your wall? The Memorise Shop, coming soon, will offer a curated selection of unique products designed to transform your digital memories into cherished physical keepsakes.

  • Personalized Photo Books: Imagine a beautiful photo book, automatically generated from your memories. Relive a year of adventures, celebrate special moments with a specific friend, or commemorate an unforgettable trip - all captured in a stunning, personalized book.
  • The Memorise World Map: Turn your living room wall into a map of your experiences! The Memorise World Map, a sleek metal masterpiece, allows you to mark all the places you've built memories with stylish magnets.
  • Many more exciting products are in the works! We're constantly brainstorming innovative ways to help you cherish your memories in tangible ways. Stay tuned for future additions to the Memorise Shop.

These beautiful keepsakes are not only a fantastic way to preserve your memories, but also a wonderful way to share them with loved ones. All proceeds from the Memorise Shop will go directly towards further development of the app, ensuring we can continue to provide you with the best possible platform for capturing and cherishing your life's moments.


2. Engaging Competitions: Share Your Stories & Win!


Get ready to add a touch of friendly competition to your memory-making journey! Memorise will soon introduce a dedicated section for exciting contests and challenges. Imagine collaborating with your friends on a memory about a shared experience, or showcasing your solo adventures.


Here's an example: Partnering with Heineken, we could host a competition themed around "Friends & Football." Submit a memory that captures the joy of friendship and celebrating the upcoming European Championship with a Heineken. Winners will not only receive a prize from Heineken, but also have the opportunity to choose a special something from the Memorise Shop!


These are just a sneak peek at the types of competitions we envision for Memorise. We're committed to creating a platform that fosters creativity, encourages storytelling, and allows you to win exciting rewards while celebrating your memories.


Stay tuned for the arrival of the Memorise Shop and Competitions! We can't wait to see how these features help you take your memory-making experience to the next level.
