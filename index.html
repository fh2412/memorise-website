What Does Memorise Help With?

We all know the struggle of collecting everyone's photos after a trip. Everyone wants all the photos, but no one ends up getting them.

I'm Florian, and I created Memorise to easily capture and share all those precious memories in one place. With Memorise, you can create a "Memory" for every trip you take and simply add your friends to it. Everyone added to a "Memory" can then enrich it with their own photos or other kinds of data.


Here's the magic: you can invite friends to join your memories! This means everyone can add their photos, write down their favorite parts, and relive the experience together, anytime.

Want to find a specific memory? No problem! Search by date, location, or simply browse through the handy map view that shows all the places you've created memories.

This is just the beginning! Memorise is a personal project fueled by the inspiration of amazing friends like you. We're always looking for new ways to improve, so feel free to share your feature ideas! Let's grow Memorise together and make sure every beautiful experience is never forgotten.

What I Want to Achieve with Memorise:

Memorise was born from a problem my friend group often faced. After realizing that the current best options for sharing photos were not satisfying for me, I got to work brainstorming my ideas. Soon, the first concept of Memorise was born, and since then, I have been constantly working on it. With a specific problem to tackle, I aim to maximize usability and create a real solution for the matter.

If you're interested in the initial trip where the idea came up, feel free to read about our amazing Kungsleden adventure. It was during a trip hiking Sweden's Kungsleden. Naturally, a trip to do a lot of thinking...

While working on the core idea, I have already stumbled upon many more features I would like to add to the app in the future. I'm currently coding away alone, so please be patient with me :)

Why is Memorise in Beta?

You might be wondering why Memorise is currently in Beta. As a one-man team (that's me, Florian!), building an app takes a lot of time and resources. Before I unleash Memorise on the world, I want to make sure it's the absolute best it can be!

Here's why Beta is important:
  • Testing, Testing, 1, 2, 3! Beta allows me to thoroughly test every corner of the app and iron out any wrinkles before a wider release. Think of it like a practice run!
  • Feedback is Gold! Beta testers like you are invaluable. Your insights and suggestions help me shape Memorise into something truly amazing.
  • Building for the Future! Right now, running Memorise at a large scale would be a bit too much for my budget. Beta helps me manage costs while the app is still under development. Those storage and server costs will become a breeze once we have a thriving Memorise community!
  • Felxibility is Key! Beta allows me to make changes and improvements quickly based on your feedback. This means the Memorise you experience will be even better when it officially launches!

So, being in Beta is not just about me - it's about creating the best possible experience for you and everyone who wants to cherish their memories with Memorise. Stay tuned for updates, and thanks for being a part of the journey!

The future of Memorise: It's all about you!

Memorise isn't just an app - it's a community in the making. We're on a journey to build a space for passionate adventurers like you, a place where you can not only store your memories but also share them, be inspired by others, and inspire them in return.

Here's a glimpse into what the future holds:
  • Evolving with you: User feedback is our guiding light. We'll constantly refine and improve existing features based on your suggestions, ensuring Memorise perfectly complements your memory-making adventures.
  • A community of memory-makers: We envision a thriving community where you can connect with fellow adventurers, share stories, and ignite inspiration for your next unforgettable experience.
  • Unleashing creativity: We're brimming with ideas for new features! Imagine: in-app activity suggestions to spark your adventures, a mobile app for on-the-go memory capturing, a shop for Memorise merchandise, in-app challenges to keep things exciting, and even AI-powered caption generation to help you perfectly capture the essence of your moments.
  • The sky's the limit: We believe there's no limit to how big Memorise can become. Together, we'll shape the future of this platform, ensuring it evolves alongside your desire to explore, create, and cherish memories.

Stay tuned for exciting updates! We can't wait to see where this journey takes us and the incredible memories we'll create together.
